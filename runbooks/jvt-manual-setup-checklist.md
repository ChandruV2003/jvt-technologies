# JVT Manual Setup Checklist

This is the short list of things that still require manual setup outside the formation and registration work.

## Already Done

- LLC formed
- EIN issued
- NJ-REG filed

## Still Manual

### Banking

- open the business checking account
- keep the account-opening confirmation in `00-admin/banking`
- add payment instructions to the invoice template after the account is open

### Business Registration Certificate

- check for the separate BRC starting around `April 22, 2026`
- save it into the formation `confirmations` folder once available

### Business Email / Sending Identity

- confirm the primary sender address you want to use for outreach
- verify that mailbox access is working for the chosen sender
- keep the sender name and signature block consistent with `JVT Technologies LLC`

### Payments

- decide whether to accept only ACH at first or also cards
- if accepting cards, choose the processor and add those instructions to the invoice template

### Contracts

- review the service agreement, SOW, and privacy addendum before sending them externally
- tighten any legal language you care about before first client use

### Outreach

- use a small reviewed wave, not bulk sending
- keep all sends human-reviewed
- do not widen outreach until the sending identity and payment path are real

## What I Do Not Need From You Yet

- bank account number
- routing number
- card processor login

Those are only needed after the account and payment setup actually exist.
