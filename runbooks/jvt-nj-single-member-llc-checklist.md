# JVT NJ Single-Member LLC Checklist

This is the practical setup path for forming `JVT Technologies` as a New Jersey single-member LLC.

This is an operating checklist, not legal advice.

## Cost Snapshot

As checked on April 20, 2026 from official NJ and IRS sources:

- New Jersey LLC certificate of formation: `$125`
- New Jersey annual report: `$75` each year
- NJ-REG business registration: `$0`
- IRS EIN: `$0`

## Recommended Filing Order

### 1. Decide the legal name before filing anything

Recommended pattern:

- `JVT Technologies LLC`

Use the official New Jersey name-availability tools before paying to file.

## 2. Form the LLC with New Jersey first

For a single-member LLC in New Jersey:

- file the `Certificate of Formation`
- fee: `$125`

Do this before getting the EIN. The IRS explicitly says to form the state legal entity first and then apply for EIN.

## 3. Get the EIN from the IRS

After the LLC is formed:

- apply for EIN directly with the IRS online
- fee: `$0`

Use only the IRS site, not third-party EIN services.

## 4. File NJ-REG

After formation:

- file `NJ-REG` online
- fee: `$0`

Important timing notes from New Jersey guidance:

- `NJ-REG` must be submitted within `60 days` of forming the new business entity
- businesses commencing operations must register at least `15 business days` before starting
- if you will collect sales tax, register at least `15 business days` before beginning that activity

## 5. Save proof documents immediately

Store these in the local documents workspace:

- certificate of formation confirmation
- EIN confirmation letter
- NJ-REG confirmation
- Business Registration Certificate once available

Recommended local path:

- `/Users/c.s.d.v.r.s./Documents/JVT-Technologies/00-admin/formation/nj-llc-setup`

## 6. Open business banking

After the LLC and EIN are in place:

- open the business checking account in the LLC name
- keep client income and expenses separate from personal activity

## 7. Set annual compliance reminders

New Jersey annual report:

- fee: `$75`
- due each year on the last day of the anniversary month of formation

Set reminders immediately after formation.

## 8. BOI / FinCEN note

As of the March 26, 2025 FinCEN interim final rule, domestic U.S. entities are exempt from BOI reporting.

That means a normal New Jersey domestic LLC currently does **not** have a BOI filing requirement under the current rule.

This can change later, so keep the official FinCEN link in the reference packet.

## 9. Outreach rule after formation

Do not widen outreach until these are all true:

- legal entity name is settled
- EIN is issued
- bank account is opened or in progress
- you have a valid business mailing address for CAN-SPAM compliance
- reviewed outbound templates reflect the actual entity name and sender identity

## Exact First Tasks

1. confirm the final entity name you want to file
2. run the NJ name-availability search
3. file the New Jersey LLC
4. get EIN from the IRS
5. file NJ-REG
6. save confirmations into the `nj-llc-setup` folder
7. open banking
8. only then widen reviewed outreach
