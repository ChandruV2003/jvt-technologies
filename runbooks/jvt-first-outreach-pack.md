# JVT First Outreach Pack

This is the practical first outbound motion for `JVT Technologies LLC`.

## Primary Wedge

- private document intelligence pilot

## Who To Contact First

- estate planning and probate firms
- employment and labor firms
- small business-law firms with document-heavy internal workflows

## Message Goal

Do not pitch “AI” in the abstract.

Pitch a private internal document assistant that:

- searches internal materials
- returns cited answers
- fits a controlled deployment path

## First Offer To Mention

- free fit call
- optional `$500` discovery and workflow review
- pilot starting at `$3,500`

## Review Rules

- no autonomous sending
- no fake personalization
- no broad blast list
- every draft should cite a real fit reason
