SAMPLE BILLING POLICY
Synthetic internal demo document for JVT Technologies.

1. Invoice Timing
Invoices are due within 30 days of the invoice date unless a matter-specific agreement states otherwise.

2. Disputed Charges
Any billing dispute should be raised in writing within 15 days of invoice delivery.
The responsible partner and billing coordinator will review disputed entries and respond within 10 business days.
Undisputed amounts remain payable by the original due date.

3. Write-Off Review
Any requested write-off above the internal threshold must be approved by the practice lead before the next billing cycle.
