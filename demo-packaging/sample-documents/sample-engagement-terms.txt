SAMPLE ENGAGEMENT TERMS
Synthetic internal demo document for JVT Technologies.

1. Confidentiality
All client matter information, work product, and internal notes must be treated as confidential.
The firm's confidentiality obligations survive termination of the engagement.
Confidential information may be shared only with personnel or vendors who need access to perform the work and who are bound by comparable confidentiality duties.

2. File Access
Client files may be accessed only through approved internal systems.
Portable copies of client materials must be encrypted when stored or transmitted.

3. Matter Closeout
At the end of the engagement, the firm will preserve the official matter file according to the records retention policy.
Any destruction of materials must follow the retention schedule and approved disposition process.
