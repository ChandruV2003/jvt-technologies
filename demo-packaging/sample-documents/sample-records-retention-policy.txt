SAMPLE RECORDS RETENTION POLICY
Synthetic internal demo document for JVT Technologies.

1. Retention Schedule
Closed matter files should be retained for seven years unless a governing rule, client instruction, or hold notice requires a longer period.

2. Hold Notices
If a legal hold or investigation notice applies, routine destruction must be suspended until the hold is released.

3. Physical And Digital Copies
Physical records scheduled for destruction must be logged before secure shredding.
Digital records must be removed from active systems and any retained archives according to the approved disposition checklist.
