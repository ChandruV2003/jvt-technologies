# Sample Solution Examples

These are honest public examples for JVT Technologies.

They are not customer case studies. They are sample scenarios based on the kinds of workflows the current demo can represent cleanly.

## Example 1: Internal Policy Search For A Law Firm

### Problem

Staff repeatedly ask the same questions about billing disputes, file access, retention rules, and engagement terms.

### JVT framing

- upload internal policies and guidance into a private document assistant
- let staff ask grounded questions in natural language
- return cited answers so the source material can be checked quickly

## Example 2: Estate Planning And Elder-Law Knowledge Assistant

### Problem

A small practice has repeat questions around intake, records, family communication, and planning-process documents, but does not want a generic public AI workflow touching that material.

### JVT framing

- private internal assistant for planning materials and office guidance
- controlled deployment conversation from the start
- room to expand into intake and follow-up workflow assistance later

## Example 3: Business-Law Or Compliance Workflow Review

### Problem

A document-heavy team loses time to manual search, inbox review, and repeated drafting work.

### JVT framing

- start with private document intelligence
- layer in triage and reviewed drafting where it actually helps
- keep people in the loop for anything that affects clients or commitments

## Example 4: Shared Inbox Review Assistance

### Problem

A small team needs help staying on top of inbound inquiries without blindly auto-responding.

### JVT framing

- import inbound mail into a local review queue
- classify direct vs promotional vs system mail
- generate draft replies locally
- keep sending human-reviewed
