# Best Demo Questions

Use short, operational questions that make verification easy.

## High-Signal Questions

- What does the engagement policy say about confidentiality after termination?
- When are invoices due and how are disputes handled?
- Which documents mention record retention requirements?
- What are the rules for storing or accessing client files?
- Which clause explains approval or sign-off requirements?

## First Three To Use

- What does the billing policy say about disputed invoices?
- What confidentiality obligations survive termination?
- Which documents mention retention requirements?

## Good Follow-Ups

- Show me the specific cited passages behind that answer.
- Which document is the strongest source for that answer?
- Are there any differences between the billing policy and the engagement terms?
- What language would an operator want to verify manually before acting?

## Questions To Avoid In A Short Demo

- very broad “summarize everything” prompts
- speculative legal advice prompts
- questions that require reasoning beyond the uploaded documents
