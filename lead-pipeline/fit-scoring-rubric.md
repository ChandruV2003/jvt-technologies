# Fit Scoring Rubric

## Score Bands

- `85-100`: strong fit, worth a tailored demo conversation
- `70-84`: good fit, worth targeted research and a careful intro
- `50-69`: possible fit, but needs more evidence before outreach
- `0-49`: weak fit for now

## Positive Signals

- clearly document-heavy workflow
- privacy-sensitive client work
- visible practice areas with precedent or policy reuse
- a team size large enough to feel search friction
- signs of operational maturity or internal tooling interest

## Negative Signals

- very broad generic business with no obvious document workflow
- no real internal team or no clear service lines
- no visible contact path
- unclear need for privacy-aware internal tooling

## Scoring Notes

- score quality of fit, not just company prestige
- prefer smaller high-fit lists over large weak lists
- use notes to explain the score so outreach stays thoughtful
- only draft outreach for leads that have a specific, defensible reason to be contacted
