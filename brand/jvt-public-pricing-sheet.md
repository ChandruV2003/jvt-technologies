# JVT Technologies Public Pricing Sheet

This is the simple external-facing price language for the first service phase.

## Free Fit Call

- 20 to 30 minutes
- used to confirm workflow fit, privacy constraints, and whether a pilot makes sense

## Discovery And Workflow Review

- starting at `$500`
- includes workflow review, document types, privacy constraints, and pilot recommendation

## Private Document Intelligence Pilot

- starting at `$3,500`
- typical pilot range: `$3,500` to `$7,500`
- scoped around one document set, one workflow, or one team

## Workflow Automation Sprint

- starting at `$3,000`
- good for intake support, triage, drafting support, or inbox operations with human review

## Internal Knowledge Assistant Sprint

- starting at `$4,500`
- good for searchable internal SOPs, policy archives, templates, and guidance

## Support

- ongoing support is available after a pilot proves useful
- production customization is quoted separately based on scope

## Payment Terms

- discovery: due on receipt
- pilots and sprints: typically `50%` upfront and `50%` at milestone or delivery

## Simple External Pricing Language

- focused demos and fit calls available
- scoped pilots for private document intelligence
- workflow automation and internal knowledge sprints available after fit review
