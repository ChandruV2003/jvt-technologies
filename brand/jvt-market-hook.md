# JVT Market Hook

Updated: 2026-05-27

## Positioning

JVT Technologies builds reviewed work systems for document-heavy teams.

The company should not lead with "AI." Lead with the operational pain:

- missed calls become intake packets
- meetings become action packets
- inboxes become triage queues
- internal documents become cited answers
- templates become review-ready drafts

AI is the engine. The buyer should feel like they are buying a cleaner operating workflow, not experimenting with a chatbot.

## Primary Hook

Send us one messy workflow. Get back one reviewed operating system.

## Plain-English One-Liner

We turn calls, inboxes, meetings, and documents into work a person can approve.

## Trust Angle

JVT is for firms that cannot afford unreviewed automation. Every first pilot has:

- one workflow
- one owner
- human review
- visible source material
- no autonomous legal, tax, medical, financial, or investment advice
- a fixed pilot price before recurring support

## Better Logo Direction

The mark should feel like an operator's seal, not a neural-network icon. The revised mark uses:

- deep pine/teal field
- warm ivory typography
- gold review brackets
- no nodes, sparks, robot shapes, or generic AI gradients

## Getting Into The Open

Near-term distribution should be concrete, not broad:

1. Publish one proof page per wedge.
2. Record a 60-90 second walkthrough for each proof page.
3. Send narrow paid-pilot offers to verified operators.
4. Post weekly before/after examples under the "reviewed work systems" hook.
5. Build referral language for IT consultants, bookkeepers, MSPs, and solo automation consultants.
6. Convert every internal JVT agent/control-panel improvement into a sanitized case study.

## First Public Proof Assets To Push

- AI receptionist paid pilot
- Meeting-to-action packet
- Inbox/document triage case study
- Workflow cleanup case study
- Document packet generator examples
