# JVT Technologies Offer Foundation

## Company Positioning

JVT Technologies designs private AI systems and workflow automation for document-heavy organizations that want practical AI gains without exposing sensitive internal material to uncontrolled public systems.

Initial focus:

- law firms
- estate planning and probate practices
- employment and labor firms
- compliance-heavy professional services
- internal legal and contract teams

## Core Offer

The first public-facing offer is a private document intelligence system that enables:

- private document search
- cited answers grounded in source text
- local-first or controlled deployment
- configurable model/provider paths
- secure internal-use workflows

## Ideal Customer Profiles

### Primary ICP

Small to midsize law firms with:

- repeat intake or review work
- internal precedent and template libraries
- billing sensitivity around partner time
- privacy concerns about public AI tools

### Secondary ICPs

- corporate legal teams with policy and contract archives
- compliance consultancies
- accounting and advisory firms with large internal knowledge bases
- specialist professional-service shops with structured but messy documents

## Messaging Pillars

### Private by design

Sensitive internal documents should stay in a controlled environment, not in a consumer workflow.

### Grounded answers, not vague summaries

Every answer should point back to supporting passages so teams can verify what the system is saying.

### Deployment that fits the client

The same product story can support:

- local-first execution on client hardware
- controlled internal deployments
- configurable provider paths based on security and cost requirements

### Practical automation, not AI theater

The goal is faster work, cleaner handoffs, and reduced search friction in real document workflows.

## Offer Ladder

### Demo / Proof of Concept

- scoped walkthrough with sample client-like documents
- decision-oriented discovery
- short validation window

### Pilot Deployment

- limited internal rollout
- target workflows selected up front
- reviewed answer quality and usage feedback

### Production Customization

- deployment hardening
- client-specific document structure and retrieval tuning
- workflow and integration customization

### Ongoing Support

- prompt and retrieval tuning
- model/provider adjustments
- operator training
- maintenance and expansion planning

## Service Packaging Notes

### JVT Private Document Assistant

This should be framed as:

- a secure internal assistant for searching and questioning internal documents
- useful for policies, precedent documents, intake materials, SOPs, and matter knowledge
- suitable for teams that need traceable answers and controlled deployment

### Packaging Guidance

- avoid claiming a finished SaaS platform
- position this as a private AI system and deployment offering
- emphasize pilot-friendly packaging and custom delivery
- keep promises tied to grounded retrieval, privacy, and workflow fit

## Proof Story

The core proof story is simple:

1. upload internal documents
2. ask a real operating question
3. receive a cited answer
4. keep the workflow private and controlled

That story should carry the early brand until real customer references exist.
