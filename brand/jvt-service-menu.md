# JVT Technologies Service Menu

## Positioning

JVT Technologies is the umbrella company.

The current flagship wedge is private document intelligence, but the broader business should present as a private AI systems and workflow automation practice for document-heavy teams.

## Flagship Offer

### Private Document Intelligence Pilot

- upload internal-style documents
- ask grounded operating questions
- return cited answers with source traceability
- keep the deployment path private, local-first, or otherwise controlled

This is the best first entry point because it is easy to demo honestly and naturally expands into adjacent automation work.

## Adjacent Services

### Internal Knowledge Assistant

- search SOPs, policy archives, engagement terms, and internal guidance
- reduce repeated questions and folder digging
- keep answers grounded in source text

### Workflow Automation Sprint

- intake assistance
- routing and triage
- drafting with human review
- internal handoff support

### Shared Inbox And Response Operations

- import inbound mail safely
- triage direct vs promotional vs system mail
- generate reviewed reply drafts locally
- keep humans in approval and sending decisions

### Secure AI Deployment Advisory

- decide what stays local
- decide what can safely use APIs
- define model, artifact, and vector storage paths
- support a practical rollout instead of a generic “AI strategy” conversation

### Custom Internal Tools

- matter or client intake tools
- searchable internal portals
- operator dashboards
- lightweight internal workflow apps that sit alongside the flagship assistant

## Offer Ladder

### Discovery And Workflow Mapping

- short workflow review
- identify document friction, privacy constraints, and pilot fit

### Focused Demo

- show one realistic use case with synthetic internal-style documents
- explain what is live now vs what would be customized

### Scoped Pilot

- one team, one matter type, one document set, or one workflow
- reviewed quality, operator usefulness, and deployment fit

### Production Customization

- client-specific tuning
- deployment hardening
- integration and workflow adaptation

### Ongoing Support

- model and provider updates
- retrieval tuning
- workflow expansion
- operator feedback loop and maintenance

## What JVT Should Avoid Saying

- avoid “we do every kind of AI”
- avoid pretending the current system is a finished SaaS platform
- avoid autonomous-outbound positioning
- avoid fake customer proof

## Simple Public Story

JVT Technologies builds private AI systems and workflow automation for document-heavy businesses.

The first flagship solution is a private document assistant for internal search, grounded answers, and controlled deployment.

That work naturally expands into internal knowledge systems, workflow automation, and operational AI tooling once the first pilot proves useful.
