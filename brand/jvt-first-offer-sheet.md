# JVT Technologies First Offer Sheet

## Positioning

JVT Technologies builds private AI systems and workflow automation for document-heavy teams that want practical gains without pushing sensitive internal material into uncontrolled public tools.

## Best First-Fit Clients

- small to midsize law firms
- estate planning and probate practices
- employment and labor firms
- compliance-heavy advisory teams
- internal legal or contract groups

## First Flagship Offer

### Private Document Intelligence Pilot

This is a scoped pilot for a document-heavy workflow where a team needs faster internal search, grounded answers, and a controlled deployment path.

## What The Pilot Does

- searches internal documents and returns cited answers
- reduces time spent digging through templates, precedents, policies, and notes
- keeps the workflow private, local-first, or otherwise controlled
- gives the client a concrete proof point instead of a generic AI strategy discussion

## What Is Included

- kickoff and workflow review
- one scoped document set or one internal workflow
- configured retrieval and cited-answer flow
- review session with operator feedback
- pilot recommendation and next-step summary

## What It Is Not

- not a mass-market SaaS product
- not autonomous legal advice
- not a broad “we do all AI” engagement
- not a giant integration project on day one

## Typical First Use Cases

- internal precedent and template search
- policy and SOP search
- matter or client-intake knowledge retrieval
- internal question-answering against approved document sets

## Commercial Path

### Private Demo And Fit Call

- free
- 20 to 30 minutes

### Discovery And Workflow Review

- starting at `$500`
- can be credited toward the pilot if the client moves forward quickly

### Private Document Intelligence Pilot

- starting at `$3,500`
- typical range: `$3,500` to `$7,500`

## Adjacent Follow-On Offers

- workflow automation sprint
- internal knowledge assistant sprint
- secure AI deployment advisory
- ongoing tuning and support

## Core Message

The first sale is not “AI.”

The first sale is a private internal assistant that helps a real team find the right internal answer faster with traceable support from the source documents.
