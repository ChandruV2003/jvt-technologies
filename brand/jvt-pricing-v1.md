# JVT Pricing V1

This is the internal JVT Technologies pricing model for the first real service phase.

It is meant to be practical and easy to quote, not over-engineered.

As of April 14, 2026, this pricing should be treated as:

- internal guidance
- starting prices
- pilot-phase pricing

Not every number needs to go on the public site yet.

## Market Scope

JVT can sell nationally across the U.S.

The main qualification filters should be:

- practice fit
- workflow pain
- privacy sensitivity
- willingness to start with a scoped pilot

Geography matters less than fit.

## Pricing Principles

- charge enough to be taken seriously
- keep the first offers easy to understand
- prefer starting prices over giant custom menus
- do not underprice pilots into a dead end
- keep payment terms simple

## Public Entry Point

### Private Demo And Fit Call

- price: free
- duration: 20 to 30 minutes
- purpose: qualify the lead, understand the workflow, and decide whether a pilot conversation makes sense

This should stay free, but short and selective.

## First Paid Step

### Discovery And Workflow Review

- starting price: $500
- payment: due on receipt
- credit policy: can be credited toward a pilot if the client moves forward within 14 days

What it covers:

- workflow review
- document types
- privacy constraints
- deployment shape
- pilot recommendation

## Flagship Offer

### Private Document Intelligence Pilot

- starting price: $3,500
- typical range: $3,500 to $7,500
- payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% at agreed milestone or delivery

What it includes:

- one scoped document set or internal workflow
- demo dataset or client-approved working set
- grounded retrieval and cited-answer flow
- deployment recommendation
- review session with operator feedback

## Adjacent Offer

### Internal Knowledge Assistant Sprint

What it includes:

- source-traceable answer behavior

## Adjacent Offer

Good for:

### Light Support Retainer

- $750/month

Good for:

- minor tuning
- review sessions
- periodic maintenance

### Active Support And Improvement Retainer

- $1,500/month

Good for:

- regular tuning
- retrieval and prompt updates
- workflow refinement
- more active support cadence

## Payment Terms

### Recommended defaults

- discovery: due on receipt
- fixed-scope sprints and pilots: 50/50
- retainers: billed upfront monthly
- invoice terms: due on receipt or Net 7

### Recommended payment method policy

- ACH preferred
- card accepted
- no surcharge policy in the first phase

## Discount Rules

- do not discount just because a lead asks casually
- do not drop below $3,000 for a real pilot unless there is a strategic reason
- if discounting, reduce scope before reducing price

## What Should Probably Stay Off The Public Site For Now

- exact monthly retainer numbers
- custom build pricing floors
- half-day / full-day advisory pricing

The public site can describe:

- discovery
- pilot
- production customization
- ongoing support

without showing every internal number yet.

## Recommended Public Pricing Language

- “Focused demos and pilot discussions available”
- “Scoped pilots for document intelligence and internal workflow automation”
- “Production customization and ongoing support available after pilot fit is confirmed”

## First Real Quote Recommendation

For the first serious qualified prospect:

- free fit call
- $500 discovery if needed
- $3,500 to $5,000 pilot quote depending on scope

That is the cleanest starting commercial motion.
