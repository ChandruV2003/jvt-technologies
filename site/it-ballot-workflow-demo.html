Agent Roles

Separate agents, clear review gates, no black box decisions.

01

Intake agent

Classifies the request, extracts dates, stakeholders, document types, and missing details.

02

Checklist agent

Builds a staff-facing milestone checklist for meeting prep, AV setup, packet status, and approvals.

03

Drafting agent

Prepares committee updates, client status emails, and internal notes from approved templates.

04

Audit agent

Records generated drafts, review status, human approvals, version IDs, and final handoff notes.

Boundaries

Operational automation, not autonomous election control.

JVT can help organize requests, draft documents, and monitor status. It should not decide eligibility, validate votes, determine winners, certify results, or send sensitive notices without an authorized review step.
