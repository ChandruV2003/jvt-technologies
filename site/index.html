Flagship Solution

The JVT Private Document Assistant

What it does

Uploads internal documents, retrieves grounded passages, and returns cited answers that can be checked quickly by an operator or attorney.

Where it fits

Policy search, engagement terms, billing rules, intake guidance, records retention, internal SOPs, and precedent-style internal knowledge.

Why this is the first wedge

Document-heavy teams feel search friction, repeat the same questions, and often cannot accept a sloppy public AI workflow for sensitive material.

What comes after

Once the document assistant proves useful, it naturally expands into internal knowledge systems, triage workflows, and higher-value automation.

1. Upload internal-style documents

Policies, engagement terms, intake notes, retention rules, or other operator-facing material.

2. Ask a practical operating question

Use the same question a staff member or partner would ask during live work.

3. Inspect citations and snippets

Review the source passages, not just the summary, before acting on the answer.

4. Move into a scoped pilot

Pick one team, one matter type, or one knowledge set and tighten the system around real usage.
