# JVT Partner Channel One-Pager

Updated: 2026-06-10

## Position

JVT helps small-business service providers add practical automation capacity
without pretending to be a giant AI platform.

The partner-facing hook:

> We build controlled intake, inbox, meeting, and document workflows for small
> businesses that need outcomes, not AI theater.

## Best Partner Profiles

- Managed service providers that already support small businesses but do not
  want to build every workflow automation themselves.
- Web design and marketing agencies that build lead-generation sites but do not
  handle post-lead operations.
- Bookkeeping, CPA, and admin-support firms that see client process breakdowns
  but are not automation implementers.
- Local business consultants and SBDCs that need practical implementation
  partners after advising a business.

## Partner Offer

JVT can support partners with:

- AI receptionist / intake dry-run and deployment planning.
- Meeting-to-action packets for client calls.
- Shared inbox and document triage.
- Workflow automation cleanup for forms, email, spreadsheets, and task queues.
- Document packet generation from existing templates and notes.

## Commercial Models To Review

- Referral model: partner introduces client, JVT sells and delivers, partner
  receives a reviewed referral fee.
- White-label model: partner owns client relationship, JVT scopes and delivers
  behind the scenes.
- Joint pilot model: one fixed-scope workflow pilot with split responsibilities.

No referral terms, white-label promises, or client commitments should be offered
until the operator approves the model.

## First Internal Validation

1. Build a no-contact shortlist of 20 potential partners.
2. Score each by overlap with small-business workflows, trust channel, and
   likelihood of needing implementation capacity.
3. Pick five for manual review.
4. Draft partner outreach only after the operator approves the target list.

## Red Lines

- No partner outreach without approval.
- No referral-fee or revenue-share promises without approval.
- No white-label commitments without a reviewed scope and delivery boundary.
- No claims that JVT is licensed for legal, tax, financial, or medical advice.
