# JVT $10k By March 2027 Plan

Updated: 2026-06-12

## Goal

Generate $10,000 in gross cash collected by March 31, 2027.

This is a faster target than a one-year goal from today, so the path needs to be service-led. Hardware/mining/trading can stay active as research tracks, but they should not consume capital until the math beats one more paid service pilot.

## Recommendation

Primary path: close small JVT paid pilots in practical service lines.

- Managed AI workflow cleanup
- AI receptionist / voice intake
- Meeting-to-action packets
- Inbox and document triage
- Document packet generation
- Website/control-panel maintenance
- Internal knowledge assistant setup

The workbook base case models roughly $12.1k expected cash by March 2027 if we close a small number of paid pilots and retain some recurring monthly support. The target average pace is about $985/month.

## Current Internal Priority Order

1. Managed AI workflow cleanup
2. AI receptionist / voice intake
3. Meeting-to-action packets
4. Inbox and document triage
5. Document packet generation

The reason for the reorder is simple: workflow cleanup is the easiest fixed-scope first-cash offer, while AI receptionist and meeting packets currently have the best proof surfaces for follow-on pilot work.

## Hardware And Speculation Rules

- Paper trading remains paper-only. It is useful for learning automation, risk controls, and reporting, but it does not count toward the $10k target.
- ASIC mining is not a buy yet. At normal residential electricity rates, S21-style economics can go negative after power and capex.
- GPU hosting is not a buy yet. It needs Linux/NVIDIA server hardware, open networking, uptime, marketplace reputation, and enough utilization to beat support burden.
- Mac hardware is better used for internal demos, transcription, vector/RAG pipelines, and client delivery than direct GPU-rental revenue.
- Token/NFT/coin ideas should wait until there is real distribution. No audience means no defensible value.
