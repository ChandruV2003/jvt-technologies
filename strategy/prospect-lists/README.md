# Offer-Specific Prospect Lists

Updated: 2026-05-20

These lists are separate from the private document assistant pipeline. They are not send-ready by default.

## Rules

- Use `priority-candidate` or `candidate-for-offer-pivot` as research inputs, not automatic send approval.
- Verify contact pages before outbound packets are created.
- Reject page-title company names, suspicious local parts, mismatched domains, and unrelated categories.
- Do not use same-day follow-ups after a large send wave unless the operator explicitly accepts the deliverability risk.

## Files

- `ai-receptionist-intake-targets.csv`: phone-heavy teams, title/mortgage/property/home-service, and intake-heavy professional offices.
- `meeting-to-action-targets.csv`: accounting, advisory, legal, consulting, and call-heavy service teams.
