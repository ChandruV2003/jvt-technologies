## Allowed Tasks

- disclose that caller is speaking with an AI assistant
- capture name, phone, email, reason for call, and callback preference
- classify request type
- create a staff review packet
- route urgent items using approved escalation instructions

## Forbidden Tasks

- no pretending to be human
- no diagnosis, legal advice, tax advice, financial advice, or medical advice
- no final appointment confirmation unless the client explicitly approves the scheduling system integration and script
- no collecting unnecessary sensitive information
- no outbound calls or SMS without separate approval
- no payment collection
