# Client Intake Checklist

- confirm the prospect is a real fit for JVT's current offer
- record legal business name and operating name
- record primary contact name, email, phone, and role
- capture website, city/state, and industry or practice area
- write the pain point in one plain sentence
- identify the first deliverable or pilot scope
- identify privacy expectations and whether documents must stay local
- confirm whether a signed NDA is needed before document sharing
- confirm whether JVT is sending a discovery invoice, pilot quote, or both
- assign a client slug before creating the local workspace
- create the client workspace only after the intake packet is complete
