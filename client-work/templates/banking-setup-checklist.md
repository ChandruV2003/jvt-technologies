# Banking Setup Checklist

Use this after LLC formation, EIN issuance, and NJ registration are complete.

## Core Documents To Bring

- certificate of formation
- IRS EIN confirmation notice
- New Jersey business registration confirmation
- government-issued photo ID for the signer
- business mailing address and phone number

## Useful Backup Documents

- business registration certificate once available
- a short one-page business description
- opening deposit source details if the bank asks
- a signed operating agreement or banking resolution if the bank requires it

## Ask The Bank

- monthly maintenance fee
- minimum balance
- ACH and wire fees
- same-day ACH support
- business debit card turnaround time
- virtual cards or employee cards
- online bill pay
- outgoing invoice payment instructions

## Immediately After Opening

- save the account-opening confirmation in `00-admin/banking`
- update invoice and proposal payment instructions
- stop using any personal account for business income or expenses
- record recurring subscriptions that should move to the business account
